# Landing Page Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to perform a systematic audit of any landing page.

## Messaging & Offer
- [ ] Headline clearly states the value proposition?
- [ ] Subheadline supports the headline and addresses pain points?
- [ ] Benefits are emphasized over features?
- [ ] Language is simple and free of jargon?

## Call to Action (CTA)
- [ ] Only one primary conversion goal per page?
- [ ] CTA is visually prominent (high contrast)?
- [ ] CTA text is action-oriented and benefit-driven?
- [ ] CTA is repeated near the bottom of long pages?

## Visuals & Layout
- [ ] Clean design with ample white space?
- [ ] High-quality, relevant hero image or video?
- [ ] No distracting links (removed header navigation)?
- [ ] Direct visual cues pointing toward the CTA (arrows, gaze)?

## Proof & Credibility
- [ ] Specific and authentic customer testimonials?
- [ ] Trust badges or security certificates visible?
- [ ] Logos of well-known partners or clients?

## Forms & Friction
- [ ] Minimal number of fields required?
- [ ] Input labels are clear and above the fields?
- [ ] No "reset" or "clear" buttons near "submit"?

## Technical & Mobile
- [ ] Fast load time (< 3 seconds)?
- [ ] Fully responsive on mobile devices?
- [ ] Links and buttons are easy to tap on mobile?
- [ ] Images are optimized and lazy-loaded?
