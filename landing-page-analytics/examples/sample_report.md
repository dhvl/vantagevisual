# Landing Page Audit: Sample Report
**Project:** Acme SaaS Platform
**Date:** March 2026
**Overall Score:** 6.5/10

---

## 🚀 Key Recommendations (Prioritized)

1.  **High Impact:** Clarify the primary headline. Currently, it's too vague ("Better results for you"). Change to: "Automate Your Sales Outreach and Close 2x More Deals."
2.  **High Impact:** Move the CTA button above the fold. It currently requires scrolling to find the first conversion point.
3.  **Medium Impact:** Add a "Trust Bar" with logos of current pilot customers to build credibility.
4.  **Low Impact:** Reduce the number of fields in the contact form from 7 to 4.

---

## 🔍 Detailed Analysis

### 1. Messaging & Clarity
- **Headline:** "Better results for you"
  - *Feedback:* Lacks specificity. Doesn't explain what the product does.
- **Subheadline:** "Our platform helps you manage your business more efficiently."
  - *Feedback:* A bit generic. Needs to highlight a unique selling point (USP).

### 2. Conversion Strategy (CTA)
- **Primary CTA:** "Learn More"
  - *Feedback:* Passive. Change to "Start Your Free Trial" or "Get a Demo."
- **Placement:** Only at the bottom of the page.
  - *Feedback:* Needs to be present in the hero section.

### 3. Trust & Social Proof
- One testimonial from "John D." with no photo or company.
  - *Feedback:* Low credibility. Needs more detail or recognizable names.

### 4. Technical Performance
- **Load Speed:** 4.2 seconds on mobile.
  - *Feedback:* Failing the 3-second benchmark. Images need optimization.
- **Mobile Experience:** Headline text overlaps on smaller screens.

---

## ✅ Next Steps
- Implement headline changes.
- Redesign hero section with an integrated CTA.
- Optimize hero image for faster loading.
